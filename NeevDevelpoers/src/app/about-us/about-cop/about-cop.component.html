Earthquake Resistant

designs of structures


There is a need to build all the buildings with earthquake resistant

design of structures. We can only improve the performance of a

building by its flexibility. With ductile detailing, all the buildings

provide more considerable deflections and enough time before the

collapse of structure. Neev also ensures to make the Earthquake

resistant design constructions of RCC building by considering

earthquake load using the equivalent static load method.
